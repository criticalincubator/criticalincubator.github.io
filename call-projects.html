Hack Weekend,
June 2-3

Call for projects

What, When, Where?

Every Sunday, a bunch of people around the globe get together to work on their post-capitalist projects, receive feedback and get inspired. We call these gatherings ‘Revolution Sunday’.

At some point, we started to wonder ‘what if every day were Revolution Sunday?’. And so the critical Incubator was born. It’s like Revolution Sunday, but longer, with a team of experts to help you shape your project and grow it out of the idea phase.

During our first ever hack weekend we will get 5-10 projects in one room with a panel of highly talented critical thinkers, designers and developers. We’ll ask the tough questions and help you formulate a strategy for how to progress beyond this weekend.

The Hack Weekend takes place June 2-3, 2018 in London. We are almost ready to announce the venue.

Who can join?

  • Projects

    Anyone with a project that is truly critical can join. That is, a project that when successful will help us move to a post-capitalist future.

    We look for a wide range of initiatives: from thought-provoking artworks, to alternative financial models and from cooperatively run factories, to tools for collective decision-making. We welcome projects that aim to instigate change and last until they achieve their goal, as well as those designed to survive the long haul. Our program is especially suitable for projects that hope to grow out of the idea phase.

  • Individuals

    Aside from projects, we'll also have a few slots for individuals who do not have a project themselves, but are keen to contribute to some else's project.

  • Advisors

    Are you a critical thinker, artist, designer, developer, or economist and would you like to spend a weekend mentoring? Please reach out to us: advisors@criticalincubator.com

Participation is free, but we are currently unable to provide travel grants to either participants or advisors. If you have any questions, please send us an email: projects@criticalincubator.com

Where do I sign up?

  • Projects

    To get a good sense of you and your project we ask you to fill out this questionnaire. Don't worry though if you can't answer these questions (yet): part of the weekend will be dedicated to helping you figure this out. However, you should be able to show us why and how your project is truly revolutionary/critical.

    The first deadline for applying is May 15th, for the remaining slots we have a second deadline on May 25th (see deadlines). Once we made sure your project is eligible, we select projects on first come first serve basis.

  • Individuals

    Individuals without projects who would like to help out can sign up by sending an email to: hello@criticalincubator.com. We only have a few slots available, so don't wait too long!
