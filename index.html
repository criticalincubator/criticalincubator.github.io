:Radical Incubator:

An incubator for radical projects striving to move us toward a postcapitalist future.

A place for radical projects to receive support, curated attention and resources from our panel of experts, which will include critical thinkers and theorists, economists, software and hardware experts. During a 3 month period we aim to grow projects from idea to implementation phase, where they will be exposed to our wider community for funding opportunities.
